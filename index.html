About the computer department

The Computer Engineering department at a polytechnic college typically focuses on preparing students for careers in the field of computer engineering. This may involve coursework in computer hardware, software design, computer networks, and other related areas. Students in the program may have the opportunity to gain hands-on experience through internships or capstone projects, as well as participate in clubs and organizations related to computer engineering. Graduates of the program may go on to work in a variety of industries, including computer hardware and software development, network administration, and information technology.


  • Coursework may include classes in computer hardware design, computer networks, software engineering, and other related areas.
  • Students may have the opportunity to gain practical experience through internships, capstone projects, or other hands-on learning experiences.
  • The program may prepare students for a variety of careers in the field of computer engineering, including hardware design, software development, network administration, and more.
  • Graduates of the program may be eligible to pursue further education, such as a bachelor's degree in computer engineering or a related field.
  • The department may offer clubs and organizations related to computer engineering, providing students with additional opportunities to learn and network with their peers.
